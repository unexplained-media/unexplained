Upsammy - Zoom



Since I want this media to have a special focus on the Dutch scene (because that is where I live and think itâ€™s pretty underrated), I thought it was a good idea to start this Dutch series with Upsammy. Came out a few months ago her first full length album, Zoom, which brings much hope for the future. Zoom is a call to observe more carefully our surroundings, to notice the smaller things that are around us and that make our lives. There is definitely a feeling of lightness to the whole album, but with a contrast of very clear melodies and blurry feeling drums. If I can pull up one hazardous metaphor, to me the album felt like a sunny winter day with the warmth of the light melodies and the coldness of the drums. Her previous projects are definitely worth a look as well!
