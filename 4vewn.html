Vewn

Victoria Vincent, also known under the name of vewn is an illustrator, but is also known for her short animation videos on youtube. I wanted to talk about her because despite her videos being quite short, around a few minutes each, you always get directly immersed in her trippy universe, with vibrant colors and absorbing perspective. The characters are usually somehow tormented, facing feelings of loneliness, depression or deal with the consequences of addiction to different elements such as new technologies or fame. However, the way she portrays those topics is often done with humor and sarcasm, definitely adding some lightness to the universe.
