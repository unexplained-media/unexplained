About



The key aspect behind Unexplained Media is the idea of diversity. There is a desire to share a diversity of artistic practices, from music to photography, illustration, painting, graphic design, the list goes on. Unexplained also focuses on the diversity of origins of the voices it shares, getting away as much as possible from the European-centered view which can be often found in media.
Unexplained is also about giving a voice to different artists and putting a light on different local scenes, trying to better understand the experience of art and its creation wherever it is.
