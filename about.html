About

I moved to the Hague in 2019, and starting to meet some the local cultural scene,
I realized that my desire to share about arts and culture was stronger than ever.
Personally being interested in a great diversity of artistic practices and styles,
I wanted through this media, to shed light on local, sometimes underground artists,
and also show a diversity of artists, from all over the world, and from eclectic practices.
